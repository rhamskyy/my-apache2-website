Computer and Network Engineering (TKJ)

Description
The Computer and Network Engineering (TKJ) program at SMKN 3 Bengkulu City focuses on building practical skills in computer systems, networks, and IT infrastructure. Students learn programming, network configuration, hardware maintenance, and troubleshooting to prepare for careers in IT, network administration, and software development. Each student takes on a specialized role to contribute to projects and real-world scenarios.
